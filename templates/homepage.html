All sunsets are beautiful, but some are particularly more beautiful than others.

This page predicts how beautiful a sunset will be based on the amount and altitude of the clouds near the selected location.
This page uses weather data from airports, also known as METARS and TAFS.
We use airport weather data because they give specific amounts and altitudes of clouds ( so that pilots can know what conditions to expect while flying).
